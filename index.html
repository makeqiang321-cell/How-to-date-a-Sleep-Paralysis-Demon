About How to date a Sleep Paralysis Demon

How to date a Sleep Paralysis Demon is a horror dating sim visual novel by Sunee built with the Ren'Py engine. You play as Dust, a bored protagonist who tries to summon a sleep paralysis demon and succeeds. What follows is not a romance—it is a fight to survive the night while a strange, unpredictable presence tests your every move.

The game revolves around two main characters: Dust, who is curious and reckless, and the demon, whose mood shifts between playful and threatening. Your choices determine how the encounter plays out, leading to multiple possible endings.

The tone moves between awkward humor and claustrophobic pressure. One moment feels like a joke; the next, the room feels unsafe. The controls are standard Ren'Py: click through dialogue, interact with room objects, and pay attention to small changes in the scene.

How to play Sleep Paralysis Demon

If you have played a Ren'Py visual novel before, the controls will feel familiar: click or press space to advance dialogue, make choices when prompted, and look for interactive objects in the room. This page serves as both a quick-start guide and a reference walkthrough for players who want to explore every branch.

First playthrough tips: Do not skip text on your first run. Lines that sound like throwaway banter can become important later. When the game offers you a choice to avoid, fight, search, wait, or look somewhere else, think about what the scene has already told you.

Headphones recommended. The web build has developing audio, and even rough sound design helps the bedroom feel less empty. If your browser blocks audio, click inside the game window after launch.

Room order matters. Experimenting with the sequence of actions—checking the window before the computer, or looking at the clock after speaking to the demon—can change what happens next. If you keep getting the same result, change the order of your interactions rather than your final choice.

Key features

Seven different endings. Finishing once is not enough. Each approach—cautious, reckless, trusting, skeptical—unlocks scenes the previous run kept hidden. Unlocking all endings requires multiple playthroughs with different choices and attitudes.

Room interaction order matters. The bed, computer, window, clock, door, phone, mirror—which one you touch first changes the outcome.

High replay value. Each run takes 20–40 minutes, and every attempt reveals something new.

The demon reacts to your behavior. What you do in the room affects the demon's attitude and dialogue.
